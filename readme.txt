Increase your web traffic with the new Google share button by including it at the bottom of each post for visitors to share content upon visit.

== Description ==

Google announced the launch of the Google Share button on April 24th 2012. They finally made it easy for people to share content among connection. This is going to be a battle with the Facebook like button.

“When your visitors come across something interesting on your site, sometimes you want to encourage a simple endorsement (like +1),” Google+ product manager Rick Borovoy wrote in a blog post introducing the new feature. “Other times, however, you want to help visitors share with their friends, right away. Today’s new Google+ Share button lets you do just that.”

The share button is geared toward increasing traffic to website if you are a marketer.
